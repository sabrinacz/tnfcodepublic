Introduction

IBMers believe in progress — that the application of intelligence, reason, and science can improve business, society, and the human condition. We lead in the creation, development, and manufacture of the industry’s most advanced information technologies, including computer systems, software, networking systems, storage devices, and microelectronics. Our worldwide network of IBM solutions and services professionals translates these advanced technologies into business value for our customers. As a Full Stack Developer, you’ll be integral to the process of invention and innovation. You’ll develop exciting solutions that change the way both IBMers and IBM clients work.


Your Role and Responsibilities

IBM CIO is hiring several Full Stack Developers. We are looking for developers who are comfortable with multi-tasking and working as part of a global team. You will also work independently and should feel confident taking ownership of your work. If you are adaptive to ambiguity and willing to change in a fast-paced environment we might be a fit for you.


Responsibilities

  • Lead code reviews and provide feedback to your peers, your manager, and the team product owner
  • Lead the review and estimation of requirements while working with your team to plan agile sprints related to application and process development roadmaps
  • Lead architecture and technical roadmap planning sessions
  • Propose implementation and architecture patterns and best practices to management and product teams
  • Develop web applications using React, Node.js, NoSQL (Cloudant)
  • Develop back-end processes and APIs required by front-end applications
  • Define necessary data models and schemas for the applications you support

Most of our teams are in Atlanta, GA; Austin, TX; Boston Metro, MA; New York, NY; Raleigh, NC; Armonk, NY; and Southbury, CT. However, since the nature of our work is hybrid, we welcome applications from other locations.

Required Technical and Professional Expertise

  • 5 years’ experience developing modern web frame works
  • 5 years development experience with Java OR Node.js
  • 3 years development experience with React
  • 1 year experience working with agile principles and executing agile methods
